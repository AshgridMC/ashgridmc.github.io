13 December 2025: .AlordXXAlord helps get the materials needed to build the map art, the map art is finished at 2:30 pm by CatMan_2012, and is the first map art on the server.

23 December 2025:  the first enderman farm was built by CatMan_2012 and EliasOttosen, BrightlyColoured helped get some materials.

24 December 2025 17:00: CatNatureCJ buys Minecraft and joins the server for the first time, later to become admin.

25 December 2025: The server continues to repeatedly crash after others travel really fast.
27 December 2025: Valoria gets griefed by a player called czosnek223344 who gets banned after he broke the chat rules of toxicity and racism. The base later gets rebuilt.
